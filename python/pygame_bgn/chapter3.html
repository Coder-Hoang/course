Chapter 3: Pygame surface

What is Pygame Surface?

Functionality: Pygame surfaces provide various functionalities to manipulate the visuals. You can draw lines, circles, rectangles, and even load external images onto the surface. Additionally, you can manipulate colors, transparency, and apply transformations like rotation and scaling.

Representation: Imagine a rectangular area on the screen. A Pygame surface represents this area, holding the visual information for what appears within its boundaries. You can create surfaces of different sizes to accommodate your game elements.
